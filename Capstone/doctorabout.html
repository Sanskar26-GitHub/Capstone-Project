About Us

The CPS mainly integrates computing with hardware control. Not only it can enhance the efficiency of system operations, but also it has the ability of monitoring and electronic device control.

The information of environment and electronic device usage collected by sensors will be sent to the Decision Making System (DMS) or the user by the existing Wireless Sensor Network (WSN) techniques, such as routing, data gathering and MAC protocols.
